My name is Cecilia and this is a picture of me wishing I had long hair.
I am an artist, currently based in Stockholm, Sweden.
I graduated with a degree in textile arts from Konstfack University of Arts, Crafts and Design in Stockholm in 2016.
Knitting is my passion but I approach the creative process from a wide range of artistic expressions, often with a humorous touch ;)
